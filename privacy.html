Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Vertex VR Video Player ("the App") is developed by Anwar Studio Official ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information.

Summary: Vertex VR Video Player collects minimal data required for in-app purchases and platform functionality. All user preferences are stored locally on your device. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation, registration, or login. However, through the Meta Horizon platform, the following data may be collected and processed:

  • User ID – Your unique Meta platform identifier (Horizon User ID) is collected through the Meta Platform SDK. This data is used to: (1) verify your in-app purchase status and entitlements, (2) ensure that your purchase of the full version is linked to your account, and (3) restore your purchase if you reinstall the App. We do not store your User ID on any external server. It is processed locally on your device and through Meta's platform services.

  • User Age Group – Age group information provided by the Meta platform, used to ensure compliance with age rating requirements.

  • In-App Purchase (IAP) Data – Purchase records processed through the Meta Horizon billing system to verify your access to the full version of the App. This includes transaction status and product entitlements.

This data is processed by the Meta Horizon platform. We do not collect or store this data on our own servers.

2. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences (display settings, playback settings) – stored in local app storage

  • Recently played files (file paths only, if the user enables this feature) – stored in local shared preferences

  • Video thumbnails – cached locally on your device for faster browsing

This data never leaves your device and is fully deleted when the App is uninstalled.

3. Network Usage

The App does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data over the network. All video files are played locally from the device storage or connected USB drives.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Meta Horizon Platform SDK for in-app purchase processing. Purchase transactions are handled by Meta's billing system. Please refer to Meta's Privacy Policy for details on how Meta processes your data. The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Camera and Sensors

The App uses the Meta Quest headset's built-in sensors (head tracking, controller input) solely for providing the VR experience. If passthrough mode is enabled, camera data is processed locally by the Meta Quest system and is not accessed or stored by the App.

6. File Access

The App requests permission to read files from device storage in order to browse and play video files. The App does not modify, upload, or share your files in any way.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Data Deletion

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All data is stored solely and exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data, including preferences, recent files, and trial information. If you need assistance with uninstalling the App or deleting your data, please contact us at psopala93@gmail.com and we will be happy to help.

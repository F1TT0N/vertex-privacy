FEATURES

🎞

180° & 360° Playback

Full support for immersive 180° and 360° video content. Experience your videos as they were meant to be seen — all around you.

👁

3D Stereo Support

Side-by-Side, Over-Under, and EAC (Equal Area Cubemap) stereo formats. Adjustable 3D strength and depth effects for the perfect viewing experience.

🎨

Advanced Color Controls

Fine-tune brightness, contrast, saturation, gamma, warmth, sharpness, and clarity. 6 built-in color profiles including Vivid, Realism, and Cinematic.

⚡

Optimized Performance

GPU-optimized shaders deliver smooth playback even on high-resolution content. Buttery smooth frame rates with minimal latency.

💻

USB & Local Storage

Browse and play videos directly from device storage or USB drives with NTFS support. Built-in file browser with folder navigation and recent history.

⚙

Full Playback Control

Grip-drag to reposition the screen, thumbstick zoom, seek controls, and passthrough background mode. Every detail is under your control.

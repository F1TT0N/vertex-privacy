Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Vertex VR Video Player ("the App") is developed by Anwar Studio Official ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information in connection with the Vertex VR Video Player application for Meta Quest.

Summary: Vertex VR Video Player collects minimal data required for in-app purchases and platform functionality through the Meta Horizon Platform SDK. All user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any external servers and do not share your data with third parties. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at psopala93@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation, registration, or login. However, through the Meta Horizon Platform SDK, the following data may be collected and processed:

  • User ID – Your unique Meta platform identifier (Horizon User ID), including your profile name and username, is collected through the Meta Platform SDK. This data is used to: (1) verify your in-app purchase status and entitlements, (2) ensure that your purchase of the full version is linked to your Meta account, and (3) restore your purchase if you reinstall the App. We do not store your User ID on any external server. It is processed locally on your device and through Meta’s platform services.

  • User Age Group – Age group category (teen or adult) provided by the Meta platform via the Get Age Category API. This data is used to ensure compliance with age rating requirements and to provide an age-appropriate experience. We do not receive your date of birth – only a general age category. This data is processed locally and is not stored on any external server.

  • In-App Purchase (IAP) Data – Purchase records processed through the Meta Horizon billing system to verify your access to the full version of the App. This includes transaction status and product entitlements. All financial information (such as credit card or payment details) is handled exclusively by Meta and is never accessible to us.

This data is processed by the Meta Horizon platform and locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on our own servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following specific purposes:

  • User ID – To identify your Meta account, verify your purchase of the full version, and restore your entitlements across app reinstalls.

  • User Age Group – To comply with Meta platform age rating requirements and ensure age-appropriate content delivery.

  • IAP Data – To process and verify your one-time purchase of the full version of the App, and to manage your access entitlements.

3. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences (display settings, playback settings) – stored in local app storage

  • Recently played files (file paths only) – stored in local shared preferences

  • Video thumbnails – cached locally on your device for faster browsing

  • Trial information (install timestamp, purchase status) – stored in local app storage

This data never leaves your device and is fully deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, license, rent, or share any user data with third parties. The only data exchange occurs between the App and the Meta Horizon platform for the purposes of verifying purchases and age group compliance, as described in this policy. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Retention

All locally stored data (preferences, recent files, thumbnails, trial information) is retained on your device only for as long as the App is installed. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all local data. Platform data (User ID, purchase records) is managed by Meta according to Meta’s Privacy Policy and their data retention practices.

6. Data Security

All data stored by the App is kept locally on your device within the App’s private storage, which is protected by the Android operating system’s sandboxing mechanism. Communication with the Meta Horizon platform for purchase verification is handled through Meta’s secure platform services. We do not operate any external servers, databases, or cloud storage.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Meta Horizon Platform SDK for in-app purchase processing and platform feature access. Purchase transactions and payment processing are handled entirely by Meta’s billing system. Please refer to Meta’s Privacy Policy for details on how Meta processes your data. The App does not integrate any other third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

8. Network Usage

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality. All video files are played locally from the device storage or connected USB drives. Network access is used only for communicating with the Meta Horizon platform for purchase verification when available.

9. Camera and Sensors

The App uses the Meta Quest headset’s built-in sensors (head tracking, controller input) solely for providing the VR experience. If passthrough mode is enabled, camera data is processed locally by the Meta Quest system and is not accessed or stored by the App.

10. File Access

The App requests permission to read files from device storage in order to browse and play video files. The App does not modify, upload, or share your files in any way.

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at psopala93@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Regardless of your location, you have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App. We do not store any personal data on our servers – all data is stored solely and exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data, including preferences, recent files, thumbnails, and trial information. No fees are charged for data deletion, and no justification is required.

For platform data managed by Meta (User ID, purchase records), please refer to Meta’s Privacy Policy for instructions on managing your Meta account data.

If you need any assistance with deleting your data or have questions about this process, please contact us at psopala93@gmail.com and we will be happy to help.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
